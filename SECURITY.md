# Security Policy

At ShortLink, the security of our users' data is of the utmost importance. 
We take a proactive approach to identifying and addressing vulnerabilities 
in our systems and applications.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We encourage the responsible disclosure of any vulnerabilities found in our systems and applications by third-party researchers. 
If you believe you have discovered a vulnerability, please notify us by emailing **batazor111@gmail.com**. 
We ask that you do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been addressed. 
We will respond to all reports within 30 days and will provide regular updates on the progress of the resolution of the vulnerability.
